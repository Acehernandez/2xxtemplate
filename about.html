Possible Uses and Distribution

  • Teaching

    This tool can be used to help teach people the different letters on their own with responsive feedback.

  • Parties Impacted

    This tool can help impact everyone who needs sign language, as well as people hoping to develop on these platforms. We reached out to the Texas school of the deaf where we realized the pressing need for a tool like this to be expanded.

  • Solving the problem

    This program DOES solve the problem of making sign language translation more accessible. Previously, technology was too expensive for people to develop on it, so good alternatives were never made.

  • Why care?

    Being able to translate one language to another effectively is important, regardless of whatever language you speak. Helping sign language translation flourish helps bring awareness to the issues today in sign language as well as bringing helping connect a global world.
